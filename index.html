A Bit About Me

If I had to describe myself in one sentence — "I'm a strategist with a storyteller's heart."

From managing social media for wellness brands to leading large-scale campaigns in the F&B space, my journey in marketing has been rooted in one truth: impactful stories build lasting brands. After completing my MBA in Marketing & Finance from Ohio University, I took a leap into the world of brand building — and never looked back.

Over the years, I've helped businesses shape their digital voice, manage projects end-to-end, and grow their communities through purpose-driven marketing. Whether it's crafting a brand narrative or leading a team to bring a campaign to life, I believe in blending strategy with soul.

Now based in Bangalore (with a soft spot for Chennai always), I continue to chase ideas that inspire action — and brands that dare to stand for something bigger.
